# ChatToSales — Marketing & Sales Strategy

## The Core Pitch (Universal)

"You already sell on WhatsApp. ChatToSales just makes it faster, smarter, and impossible to forget a customer."

Never lead with technology. Lead with the pain they already feel.

---

## Strategy 1: The Demo-First Approach

Don't explain. Show.

Walk into a market, find a trader, and say: "Send me a voice note of your products and prices." Then show them their store link 3 minutes later. That's the pitch. The store link sells itself — every trader wants a "website" but thinks it costs N500k.

**Script:**
> "Oga/Madam, if I can give you your own website with all your products — and customers can order from you on WhatsApp just by clicking one link — would you use it?"
>
> They say yes. Every time.
>
> "Send me a voice note of your top 10 products and prices."
>
> Set them up in 3 minutes. Send the store link. Done.

## Strategy 2: By Trader Type

### Mama Caro — Provisions/Food Seller (Bodija, Oja Oba, Mile 12)

**Her pain:**
- Customers call asking "you get Milo?" — she's too busy to check and respond to everyone
- She forgets who owes her money every Friday
- She broadcasts "new stock" by forwarding messages one by one to 50 contacts
- She loses customers because she forgot to follow up

**What to say:**
> "Mama, you know how customers message you on WhatsApp asking for price, and sometimes you forget to reply? This thing will reply for you. It knows all your products and prices. When they order, it tells you. When they owe you, it reminds them. And on Friday, it tells you exactly who owes you and how much."

**The hook:** "WHO OWES ME" — type it and see your full debt book. Every provisions seller has money outside. This feature alone sells the app.

**Demo order:**
1. Show her the WHO OWES ME feature
2. Show her the broadcast ("message all your customers at once")
3. Show her the weekly report ("every Monday you get your sales summary")

**Demo order:**

And when a customer says they want to buy, it creates an order, you confirm it, and it sends them your bank details. No more back and forth."

**The hook:** Automatic price replies + order management. Phone sellers hate repeating prices. This saves them 2-3 hours daily.

**Demo order:**
1. Send "how much is iPhone 15?" — show auto catalogue response
2. Place a test order — show the full flow
3. Show the credit tracker — "you know Bro Tunde who owes you from last month?"

## Strategy 3: Channel-Specific Marketing

### WhatsApp Status Marketing
- Post daily tips for traders: "3 ways to never forget a customer order again"
- Show before/after: messy WhatsApp chat vs clean ChatToSales order flow
- Post testimonial clips from early users
- Share the Status Kit output — "this was generated in 3 seconds"

### Market Walk-In (Guerrilla)
- Go to markets with a phone
- Find traders who are visibly busy/overwhelmed
- "Madam, can I show you something for 2 minutes?"
- Set them up on the spot, free trial
- Their neighbouring traders will ask "what's that?"

### WhatsApp Group Marketing
- Join trader association WhatsApp groups
- Share the store link of a successful trader
- "This trader got 15 orders from WhatsApp last week using this free tool"
- Don't sell — let them ask

### Instagram/Twitter
- Target the young entrepreneur audience
- "Stop screenshotting your orders" campaign
- Show the dashboard — professional, clean, makes them feel like a real business
- Influencer gifting — give free Alatise tier to 10 popular small business owners

### Referral Program
- "Refer 3 traders, get 1 month free"
- Traders trust traders. Word of mouth in the market is everything.
- The trader's store link itself is marketing — customers see "Powered by ChatToSales"

## Strategy 5: Pricing Psychology

### The 90-Day Free Trial
- No credit card. No commitment. Just start.
- "Use everything free for 90 days. If it doesn't make you more money, delete it."
- This removes ALL objections. The product sells itself once they use it.

### The "Pay Less Than Your Daily Bread" Anchor
- Oja tier: N1,500/month = N50/day
- "You spend more than N50 on bread every day. This tool makes you more than N50 in its first hour."

### The ROI Argument
- "If this tool recovers ONE forgotten debt of N5,000, it has paid for itself for 3 months."
- "If ONE customer orders because of a broadcast you sent, the subscription pays for itself."
- "You're losing more money WITHOUT this tool than the N1,500 it costs."

## Strategy 8: First 100 Users Plan

1. **Week 1-2**: Sign up 10 traders personally (market walk-ins). Set them up yourself. Get feedback.
2. **Week 3-4**: Ask those 10 to refer 2 each. Offer free Oja tier for referrals. Target: 30 traders.
3. **Month 2**: Join 5 trader WhatsApp groups. Share success stories. Target: 50 traders.
4. **Month 3**: Launch referral program. WhatsApp Status marketing daily. Target: 100 traders.
5. **Month 4**: First paid conversions from 90-day trial expiring. Measure conversion rate.

### Key Metric
If 30% of free trial users convert to paid, you need 333 free users to get 100 paying customers.

At N1,500/month average: 100 paying customers = N150,000/month recurring revenue.
